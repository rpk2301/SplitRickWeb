Groups

One balance per group. Nothing to reconcile later.

Spin up a group for your apartment, a trip, or anything else you split money on. Invite people with a six-digit code or a shared link. SplitRick keeps a running balance so you always know where everyone stands.

  • Filter by who you owe, who owes you, or what's already settled
  • Group photos and names you can actually tell apart
  • Join a group in under ten seconds
Ledger

Split equally, by percent, or by exact amount.

Drop in the amount, pick who paid, and split it however makes sense. Rent goes by percent. Groceries split equally. The tickets went 70/30. SplitRick does the math and shows exactly what each person owes.

  • Categories, descriptions, and a receipt photo per expense
  • Edit or delete without wrecking anyone's running balance
  • See who lent what at a glance — no spreadsheets
Settle Up

Pay what's owed. Zero out the group.

The Settle tab shows the fewest payments needed to square up every group you're in. When the cash actually changes hands, record the payment and everyone's balance clears.

  • Simplified transfers across all your groups at once
  • Push notifications when someone pays you back
  • Group-level or person-level settlements — your call
