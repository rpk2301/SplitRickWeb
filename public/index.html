About Splitrick

Splitrick is a secure expense sharing and group finance platform that lets users easily split bills, track spending, and settle payments in real time. Our mission is to make financial collaboration between friends, families, and roommates effortless, transparent, and privacy‑focused.

Security First

We take security seriously—your personal and financial data is encrypted, protected, and never shared with anyone. Splitrick ensures that only you and your chosen group members can view expense information.

Built by Users, for Users

Splitrick is built by a team of engineers with experience in fintech and cloud infrastructure, ensuring a reliable experience for every user.

Free, Always

Splitrick is—and will always be—completely free to use. We believe that managing shared expenses shouldn’t come with extra costs or subscriptions. There are no hidden fees, no premium tiers, and no ads—just a simple, transparent way to keep group finances organized. Our goal is to make fair expense tracking accessible to everyone, whether you’re splitting rent, planning trips, or managing day-to-day bills with friends and family.
